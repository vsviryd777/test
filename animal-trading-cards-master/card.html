Amur tiger

The population of Amur tiger today amount to less than 500. The IUCN lists this subspecies as "critically endangered".

  • Subspecies: Siberian tiger / Amur tiger
  • Body lenght: 8ft 10in
  • Weight: 441-701 lb
  • Lifespan: about 15 years
  • Habital: East of Russia and ajacent area of North Korea and China
